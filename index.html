About Me

I'm a passionate Computer Engineering student at Brigham Young University with a focus on robotics and embedded systems. My journey in engineering has led me to explore the fascinating intersection of hardware and software, where I love solving complex problems through innovative solutions.

My technical interests span from low-level embedded programming to high-level software architecture and machine learning applications in robotics. I'm particularly drawn to projects that involve real-time systems, computer vision, robotic control systems, and sensor fusion.

When I'm not coding or tinkering with hardware, you can find me biking Utah's beautiful mountains, wakesurfing on the lake, or working on personal robotics and automation projects in my home lab. I believe that the best engineers are lifelong learners, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges and expand my skillset.
